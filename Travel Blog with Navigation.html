🏔️ Manali Adventure

Manali welcomed me with cool air, snow peaks and peaceful valleys. Walking through pine forests felt refreshing.

At Solang Valley, I tried paragliding and saw breathtaking aerial views. Rohtang Pass was fully covered in snow, making it magical.

Local cafes served hot Maggi and tea which made the experience even more special.

Beach

🏖️ Goa Trip

Goa is full of energy with beaches, music and delicious seafood. The vibe is relaxing yet exciting.

I enjoyed water sports like jet skiing and parasailing. Watching sunset at Baga Beach was unforgettable.

Nightlife with music, lights and beach parties made every moment lively.

Desert

🏜️ Rajasthan Safari

Rajasthan showed royal culture and desert beauty. Jaisalmer's golden forts were amazing.

Desert safari on camel across sand dunes during sunset was the best part.

Evening cultural dance and music under stars created a lifetime memory.
